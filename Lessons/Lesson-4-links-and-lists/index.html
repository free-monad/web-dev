If you click on any of the links in the aside, you will swiftly be taken to another page of the site or two an external site or a particular resource. Try out each link and then inspect the pages or resources that you are taken to. Try to understand how they work.

Notice that the a tags are responsible for making the hyperlink. The text inside the a tag is what is displayed. The attribute href is what determines where you are sent to when you click the link. Sometimes you are sent to another page on the same website. Sometimes you are sent to other places on the web.
